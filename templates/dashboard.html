        Your Employability Score

        This is a single 0-10 score averaging the 3 areas that most directly affect whether you get shortlisted at all: ATS Score (can automated screening software even read your CV), Skills (how in-demand and well-evidenced your skills are), and Experience (your track record and measurable impact). Tap any of the three cards below for the specific reasoning behind that score, including exactly what would raise it.

        We still check 5 other areas — Documentation, Qualifications, Market Fit, Credibility, and Progression — and they're not going anywhere; find them under Full Breakdown below the 3 main cards.

        In really simple terms: think of it like your 3 most important subjects on a report card, averaged into one grade out of 10. Your other subjects still get marked and still matter — they're just kept in a separate section so the headline number stays focused on what moves the needle most.
