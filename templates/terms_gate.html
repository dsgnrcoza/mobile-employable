Quick, plain-language basics. No fine print games.

What this app actually does

Ploy reads your CV and any documents you upload, then gives you an honest score of how ready you are for the job market — plus specific steps to improve it.

It works as hard as you do

This app works with you exactly as much as you're willing to work with it. The more complete and honest the information you give it, the more accurate and useful your results will be. Half-effort in means half-useful out — that's on you, not a flaw in the app.

Your uploads are private and secure

Everything you upload is stored securely and kept private. We never sell it, share it, or hand it to recruiters, employers, or anyone else. It stays yours, for as long as you have an account — see our Privacy Policy for the full details.

The basics we expect from you

Only upload documents that are actually yours. Don't use this app to impersonate anyone, break the law, or cause harm. Keep your login details to yourself. That's really it.
